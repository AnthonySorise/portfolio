About Anthony

I love creating things

From a young age, I've been driven by the enjoyment I find with the creative process. I've explored this passion in many ways, which initially lead me to the cherished experiences of working on audio production for several dozen AAA games, including XCOM II, God of War: Ascension, and Call of Duty : Black Ops II. As a result of my proximity to the video game industry, and my interest in game development, I developed a strong curiosity for computer programming. This curiosity turned into fascination as I taught myself C# and Unity in 2012. I discovered that my enthusiasm for creating things and problem solving translates very well to development. With a strong desire to deepen my understanding, I completed the LearningFuze accelerated web development program in 2017 where I enjoyed over 800 hours of training using a variety of technologies. I've since had the opportunity to expand my experience at several companies as a web developer. I'm currently employed at Breakthrough Technologies as a software engineer.

A Fallout themed match game with additional mechanics including items, health points, and damage resistance.
